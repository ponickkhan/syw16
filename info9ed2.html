About:

IEEE BDS SYW Congress 2016 is the largest annual event of IEEE Bangladesh Section. IEEE Student Branch University of Dhaka is the host of the 4th IEEE BDS SYWC 16. The Congress is divided into three tracks: Student, Young Professional and Women in Engineering.
Scheduled date of the congress: 04 November, 2016 (Friday)

Venue:

Nabab Nawab Ali Senate Building, University of Dhaka.
